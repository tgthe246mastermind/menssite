Preparation Phase

Self-Assessment Tools

Preliminary Coaching

Initiation Phase

Immersive Retreat

A carefully designed environment away from daily distractions.

Experiential Processes

Break through limiting patterns through powerful group and individual exercises.

Integration Phase

Follow-up Sessions

Practice Assignments

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this program different from other personal development courses?

+

Our program uniquely combines experiential learning, ongoing community support, and a structured integration process. Rather than just providing information, we create transformational experiences that lead to lasting change, supported by a brotherhood committed to each other's growth.

How do I know if I'm ready for this experience?

+

If you're feeling called to deepen your self-understanding, improve your relationships, or discover greater purpose and meaning, you're likely ready. We recommend scheduling a call with one of our enrollment advisors who can help you assess if the timing is right for you.

What kind of results can I expect?

+

While each person's journey is unique, participants regularly report greater emotional awareness, improved communication skills, deeper connections with others, enhanced leadership capabilities, and a stronger sense of purpose and direction in life.

How much time should I commit to the program?

+

The core intensive weekend requires a full 3-day commitment. The preparation phase involves 2-3 hours per week for 4 weeks. The integration phase includes weekly meetings (2 hours) for 8 weeks. Beyond this, participation in the community is flexible based on your availability and goals.
