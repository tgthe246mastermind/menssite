Frequently Asked Questions

You can participate in our programs by registering through our website or contacting us directly. Many of our programs have regular start dates throughout the year, and we're happy to help you find the right program for your needs.

The New Warrior Training Adventure is our flagship program, a transformative weekend experience that helps men examine their lives, connect with their authentic selves, and develop emotional intelligence and leadership skills in a supportive community environment.

We have support groups, called Integration Groups (I-Groups), in many locations around the world. Contact us with your location, and we'll help connect you with a group near you or provide information about starting a new group.

We welcome volunteers and supporters! There are many ways to get involved, from helping with events and programs to providing financial support. Contact us for more information about current volunteer opportunities and ways to contribute.
